# Product Overview

AyurPulse is an intelligent EMR (Electronic Medical Records) and EHR (Electronic Health Records) system designed specifically for modern Ayurvedic medical practices.

## Core Purpose

Provides digital infrastructure for Ayurvedic healthcare delivery, connecting doctors (Vaidyas) and patients through a comprehensive health management platform.

## Key Features

- **Dual Portal System**: Separate interfaces for doctors and patients
- **Prakruti Analysis**: Constitutional assessment and tracking
- **Vikruti Monitoring**: Current health state and dosha imbalance tracking
- **Panchakarma Management**: Multi-stage detoxification cycle tracking with Purvakarma, Pradhanakarma, and Paschatkarma phases
- **Clinical Records**: Digital prescriptions, diagnosis, and treatment history
- **Predictive Wellness**: AI-driven health forecasting based on dosha profiles
- **Appointment System**: Scheduling and notification management
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Patient retention, revenue tracking, and dosha distribution insights

## User Roles

- **Doctors**: Clinical workflows, patient management, analytics, Panchakarma oversight
- **Patients**: Health journey tracking, appointments, personalized recommendations, treatment history

## Domain Context

Built for Ayurvedic medicine, incorporating traditional concepts like:
- Tridosha theory (Vata, Pitta, Kapha)
- Prakruti (constitutional type)
- Vikruti (current imbalance state)
- Panchakarma therapy protocols
- Ayurvedic diet and lifestyle recommendations
